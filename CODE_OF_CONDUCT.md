# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

TypeScript Mastery is a learning-focused open-source project. We are committed to a respectful, welcoming environment for everyone who participates, regardless of experience level, background, identity, or communication style.

## Expected behavior

Please:

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before making assumptions.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Welcome beginner questions and different approaches to learning.
- Respect privacy and do not share personal information without permission.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, trolling, sexualized language, deliberate intimidation, or publishing private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the repository maintainer through the contact details on the [repository home page](./README.md). Reports will be handled respectfully and as confidentially as practical.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct. The goal is to protect a constructive learning environment.
